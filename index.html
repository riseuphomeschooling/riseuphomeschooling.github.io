About Us

The most difficult questions that aspiring homeschooling parents struggle with are choosing the right curriculum and striving to impart it to children without any formal training of teaching. Information on the internet is very unorganized and either discusses personal chronicles of homeschooling parents or provides such a huge variety of curriculum options that parents often end up more confused. Furthermore, the prices of books customized for homeschooling are so high that it eliminates one of the major benefits of homeschooling i.e. affordability.
Prime Homeschool offers Montessori Curriculum and continuously coaches parents online on how to impart that curriculum to their children from play group to nursery. We break down the curriculum into Weekly Parts and setup Online Meetings with parents to coach them on how to instruct children creatively and effectively. This is carried out step by step on a weekly basis to accommodate manageable pace for both parents and children.

Weekly Lesson Plan

Every week, we share a Weekly Lesson Plan with parents which includes Supplementary videos,content ,websites, educational games and activities.
